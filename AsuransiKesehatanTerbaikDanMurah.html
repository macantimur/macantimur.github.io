Asuransi Kesehatan Terbaik Dan Murah Ndi a tunyere saiti ruo oru na a oru-ndabere site nkwekorita na ha na-ekere oke ike ego. The Private Health Insurance Ombudsman nwekwara-aru oru a free website nke na-enye ohere na-eji oku icho na tunyere onwe ike insurers 'na ngwaahia, nke na-agunye ozi na price na larii nke cover. Otutu ihe onwe ike mkpuchi na Australia na-usoro Private Health Insurance Act 2007. Complaints na akuko nke nkeonwe ike ulo oru na-ruru site na otu nooro onwe ha goomenti, na Private Health Insurance Ombudsman. The ombudsman ebiputa kwa afo akuko na-akowaputa nomba na odidi nke mkpesa kwa ike enweta ego tunyere ha ahia oke. The onwe ahu ike na usoro ke Australia na-aru oru a "obodo fim" ndabere, adiru premiums adighi idi iche iche nani n'ihi na nke a onye gara aga ogwu ihe mere eme, ugbu a ala nke ahu ike ma o bu (-ekwu) ha afo (ma hu ndu Health Cover n'okpuru). Asuransi Kesehatan Terbaik Dan Murah Ego na-isiokwu wetara a oge nchere nke ruo onwa 12 na uru n'ihi na o bula onodu ahuike na ihe iriba ama na ihe mgbaama nke nke adi n'oge onwa isii agwucha na ubochi onye mbu wee si mkpuchi. Ha nwekwara ikike imanye a 12-onwa na-echere oge maka uru maka omume metutara ihe obstetric onodu, na a 2-onwa na-echere oge niile uru ndi ozo mgbe mmadu mbu na-ewe nani mkpuchi. Ego nwere uche iji belata ma o bu wepu di otu ahu na-eche oge na onye ikpe. Ha na-free ghara imanye ha ka ha na-amalite, ma nke a ga-etinye ndi di otu ahu a enweta ego n'ihe ize ndu nke "oghom nhoro", na-adota a disproportionate nomba nke otu ndi ozo ego, ma o bu site na odo-miri-mbiba otu ndi nwere ike n'uzo esonyela ozo ego. O ga-adota ndi mmadu na edude ogwu onodu, bu ndi puru na o garaghi e si insurance mgbe niile n'ihi na nke gonahu uru n'ihi na onwa 12 n'ihi na agwa Oma. The uru ugwo maka onodu ndi a ga-ike nsogbu na premiums niile ego si otu, na-eme ufodu na dobe ha otu, nke ga-eduga n'ihu adahade na premiums, na a na ajo okirikiri nke elu premiums-ahapu ndi otu ga ensue.
